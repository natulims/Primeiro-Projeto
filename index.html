about me

Hello! I'm Natalia (or natu), a brazilian illustrator and Front-End developer! I love web design and learn new things to improve my skills. I've always worked as a freelance illustrator and now I'm learning how to be a good Front-End developer.

I already made drawings for websites, games and clothes. This is my first time dealing with web design and creating my own websites.

I like to be able to share what I do with everyone! My goal is to get better creating my own websites and learn more about graphic design. But I'm open to explore and try out new things that I wouldn't even thought about before.
